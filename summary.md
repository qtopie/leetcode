
## How to solve coding problems elegantly?

Think before coding. You just need to complete it in time, not shortest time to find solution

Three Steps:
1. Understand the problems (context, what's the problem your algorithm solves? input/output, examples)
2. Model your algorithm with Math and computer science (how to modeling? find features)
3. Conduct graph/psudeocodes to help you build the bridge between model and codes, them implement it with codes
